Privacy Policy

Family Line (패밀리 라인) · EJSoft

Family Line (the “App”) is a family tree and family schedule management application provided by EJSoft (the “Company”). The App does not require any account registration or sign-in, and the family information you enter is, in principle, stored only on your device. The Company does not collect or store your family information on any server.

This policy explains the information the App processes and the information that may be collected through third-party tools.

1. Information We Collect and Use

  1. Information stored on your device (not transmitted to any server)
    The following information that you enter in the App is stored only on your device and is not sent to the Company’s servers or any external party.
    • Family member details: name, relationship, gender, and anniversaries such as birthdays, memorial days, and wedding anniversaries
    • Family schedules and notification settings
    • Member photos (if you add them)
    • Linked contact information: contacts (name, phone number) that you choose to link to a family member. This information is used only on your device and is not transmitted externally.
  2. Information that may be collected through third-party tools (SDKs)
    The App uses the following third-party tools to provide and improve the service and to display ads. Information may be collected and processed according to each provider’s policy.
    • Google AdMob (advertising): advertising identifier, device information, approximate location, ad-interaction data, etc. (to display and measure ads). Ads are not shown after purchasing “Remove Ads.”
    • Google Firebase Analytics (usage analytics): app usage, screen views, app version, device type, etc. (to improve the service)
    • Google Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics): diagnostic information on app crashes, device state, app version, etc. (to improve stability)
    • Google Play Billing: the “Remove Ads” in-app purchase is processed by Google Play. The Company does not store payment information.

2. Purpose of App Permissions

The App uses the following permissions to provide its features. Information accessed via these permissions is processed on the device within the scope of the stated purpose.

  1. Read Contacts: to link family members with device contacts
  2. Notifications: to show reminders for birthdays, memorial days, and schedules
  3. Storage: to back up/restore data and to add member photos
  4. Biometrics: for the app lock (identity verification) feature
  5. Exact Alarm: to deliver anniversary reminders at the scheduled time

Permissions are requested only when the related feature is used, and you can change or revoke them at any time in your device settings.

3. Retention and Deletion

  1. Family information, schedules, photos, and similar data you enter are deleted from the device when you delete them in the App or uninstall the App.
  2. Backup files you create via “Export/Share” are saved to the location you choose, and you are responsible for managing and deleting those files.
  3. The retention period and deletion of information collected by third-party tools follow each provider’s policy.

4. Provision to Third Parties

The Company does not provide or sell your family information to third parties. However, the third-party tools listed in Section 1.2 process information according to their own policies, which you can review below.

  • Google (AdMob · Firebase · Play Billing) Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data in its ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children under the age of 14 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You may view, edit, or delete the information you entered at any time within the App. Ad personalization can be adjusted in your device’s ad settings (reset advertising ID / opt out of personalized ads).

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised in line with changes in applicable laws or the service. We will announce any changes through the App or the distribution channel.
